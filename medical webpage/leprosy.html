Leprosy Disease Information

Symptoms

  • Appearance of pale or reddish skin patches with reduced sensation
  • Numbness or tingling in affected areas
  • Loss of eyebrows or eyelashes
  • Thickened, dry, and stiff skin
  • Ulcers or sores that don't heal
  • Muscle weakness or paralysis

Precautions

  • Seek early diagnosis and treatment
  • Complete the full course of prescribed medications
  • Practice good hygiene, including regular handwashing
  • Avoid close and prolonged contact with individuals with untreated leprosy
  • Protect any open wounds or sores from exposure and infection
  • Follow recommended self-care practices provided by healthcare professionals
