Diabetes Information

Symptoms of Diabetes

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Extreme hunger
  • Fatigue or weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores or infections
  • Tingling or numbness in the hands or feet
  • Recurrent infections, such as urinary tract infections
  • Irritability or mood changes

Precautions for Diabetes Management

  • Maintain a healthy and balanced diet, emphasizing whole grains, lean proteins, fruits, and vegetables
  • Avoid sugary and high-carbohydrate foods
  • Control portion sizes and monitor carbohydrate intake
  • Engage in regular physical activity and exercise
  • Monitor blood glucose levels regularly
  • Take prescribed medications as directed by healthcare professionals
  • Follow a consistent meal and medication schedule
  • Manage stress levels through relaxation techniques
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption
  • Get regular check-ups and screenings for complications
