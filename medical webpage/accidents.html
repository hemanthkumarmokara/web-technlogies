Accident Information

Symptoms of Accidents

  • Pain or discomfort
  • Bleeding or bruising
  • Swelling or inflammation
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Broken bones or fractures
  • Cuts, wounds, or lacerations
  • Burns or scalds
  • Head or neck injuries
  • Back or spinal cord injuries

Precautions for Accident Prevention

  • Follow traffic rules and wear seat belts when driving or riding in vehicles
  • Observe safety protocols in workplaces and use protective equipment
  • Secure and maintain safety features in homes, such as smoke detectors and fire extinguishers
  • Use caution when handling sharp objects, chemicals, or hot surfaces
  • Avoid distractions while walking, driving, or operating machinery
  • Practice safe handling and storage of hazardous materials
  • Keep floors and walkways clear of obstacles
  • Use proper lifting techniques to prevent strain or injury
  • Take regular breaks to prevent fatigue and maintain focus
  • Stay alert and aware of your surroundings
