Ebola Disease Information

Symptoms of Ebola

  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Muscle and joint aches
  • Weakness and fatigue
  • Sore throat
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Rash
  • Impaired kidney and liver function
  • Bleeding (internal and external)

Precautions for Ebola Prevention

  • Wash hands frequently with soap and water or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer
  • Avoid close contact with individuals displaying symptoms of Ebola
  • Avoid contact with blood or bodily fluids of infected individuals
  • Do not touch the body of someone who has died from Ebola
  • Practice safe burial procedures
  • Avoid contact with wild animals, especially bats and monkeys
  • Practice safe sex or abstain from sexual activity during an outbreak
  • Follow infection control procedures in healthcare settings
  • Stay informed about the latest guidelines and recommendations
